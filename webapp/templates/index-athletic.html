How Our Shoe Matchmaker Works

Our advanced matching engine analyzes thousands of lab-tested metrics to find your perfect running shoe alternative.

1. Comprehensive Data Analysis

We evaluate key performance metrics including cushioning, drop, weight, flexibility, and energy return to ensure optimal match quality.

2. AI-Powered Matching

Our machine learning model, trained on expert ratings, understands the nuanced relationships between different shoe characteristics.

3. Personalized Results

Every recommendation is scored and ranked based on how closely it matches your selected shoe's performance profile.

The result? You get scientifically-backed shoe recommendations that feel like they were picked by a expert running store specialist.
