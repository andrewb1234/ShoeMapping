SYSTEM OVERVIEW

Finding the perfect running shoe can be overwhelming. We cut through the marketing hype by finding shoes that are mathematically and physically similar to your favorites.

THE DATA

Comprehensive metric extraction from laboratory testing including cushioning profiles, torsional rigidity, energy return coefficients, and weight distributions.

THE ENGINE

The recommendation engine was trained against AI critique to deeply understand shoe performance, with every match validated and cross-referenced against distinct mechanical feature clusters.

THE RESULTS

When you select a shoe, the system instantly calculates its similarity to hundreds of other models. We then validate the output against feature clusters to ensure your top recommendations structurally match your selection.
