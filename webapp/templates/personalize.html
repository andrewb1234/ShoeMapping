How to export from Strava
  1. Log in to Strava.com on a desktop browser.
  2. Hover over your profile picture and select Settings.
  3. Click the My Account tab.
  4. Scroll to "Download or Delete Your Account" and click Get Started.
  5. Select Request your archive.
  6. Wait for an email (can take hours to days) with a download link.
  7. Unzip the file and select activities.csv.
